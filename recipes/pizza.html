Pepperoni
Delicious looking and even more delicious tasting pizza, that was cooked traditional way

Ingredients

  • Dough 500g
  • Cheese 300g
  • Tomato 100g
  • Sauce 100g
  • Sausages 300g

Steps

  1. Make a dough rounded and thinner
  2. Put cheese on top
  3. Slice tomatoes and put them on top
  4. Put sauce on top
  5. Slice sausages and put them on top
