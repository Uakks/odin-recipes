Receipt

Whopper
Big, fat and satisfying burger with lots of products in it

Ingredients

  • Bread - 2 slices
  • Cheese 100g
  • Meat 300g
  • Tomato 100g
  • Sauce 100g
  • Cucumbers 50g
  • Lettuce 50g

Steps

  1. Take one slice of bread
  2. Slice tomatoes and cucumbers
  3. Put meat on one of the slices of bread
  4. Put cheese on top
  5. Put sliced tomatoes on top
  6. Put sauce on top
  7. Put lettuce on top
  8. Put sliced cucumbers on top
  9. Put second slice of bread on top
